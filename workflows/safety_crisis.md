# safety_crisis

## Goal
Prioritize immediate user safety when self-harm, suicide, violence or acute danger is mentioned.

## Steps
1. Acknowledge the user directly and calmly.
2. Ask the user to move away from means of harm and toward another person.
3. Encourage contacting local emergency services or a crisis hotline.
4. Keep the user engaged with a very small response request.

## Boundaries
- Do not debate whether the user's pain is valid.
- Do not promise secrecy.
- Do not provide methods, means or encouragement for harm.
